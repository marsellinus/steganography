About Spectrograms

Spectrograms show how the frequency content of the audio changes over time. Differences in the spectrograms may reveal where steganographic content has been embedded. Minimal visible differences indicate effective steganography.

About Frequency Spectrum

The frequency spectrum shows the distribution of audio energy across different frequencies. Effective steganography should maintain a similar frequency distribution between the original and stego audio. Significant differences in specific frequency bands could reveal the presence of hidden data.
