Audio Analysis Information

This tool allows you to compare original and stego audio files to assess steganography quality. The analysis includes waveform comparison, spectrogram analysis, frequency analysis, and quality metrics like SNR (Signal-to-Noise Ratio) and PSNR (Peak Signal-to-Noise Ratio).

Supported Audio Formats:

  • WAV (.wav) - Recommended for best accuracy
  • FLAC (.flac) - Lossless audio format
  • MP3 (.mp3) - May have compression artifacts
  • OGG (.ogg) - Compressed audio format
